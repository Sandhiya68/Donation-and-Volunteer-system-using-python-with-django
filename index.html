Volunteer

A volunteer is someone who offers their time, skills, or services without expecting financial compensation, typically to help a cause, organization, or community. Volunteering can involve a wide range of activities, and volunteers play a crucial role in supporting social, humanitarian, environmental, and community-based projects.Picking up donations from donors.
